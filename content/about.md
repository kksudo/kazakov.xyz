[Kirill Kazakov](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kazakovk/) is a Senior Platform & DevOps Engineer with 17 years in IT (9+ in Kubernetes, AWS, Terraform, and CI/CD). He focuses on developer experience, self-service workflows, and observability — building reusable CI/CD platforms for 100+ microservices (Justt, FinTech), integrating the Argo family as GitOps core, and designing observability stacks for enterprise clients. He delivers DevOps as a Service at GlobalDots (FinTech, AI Security, Government) and serves as CTO at Aura X Capital (DeFi, 350k+ users). He authored 30+ technical publications, and taught 500+ students as an OTUS instructor.

He teaches the [Kubernetes-Based Infrastructure Platform](https://otus.ru/lessons/infrastrukturnaya-platforma-na-osnove-kubernetes/) course at Otus, creates educational programs for [SkillFactory](https://skillfactory.ru/devops-engineer), and writes for [Medium](https://kksudo.medium.com/), [Dzone](https://dzone.com/users/4670608/kk-sudo.html), [HackerNoon](https://hackernoon.com/u/kksudo), and [Habr](https://habr.com/ru/users/kksudo/). He’s an active member of the **"I Want to Know Everything"** and **"Happy to Share What I’ve Learned"** communities.
